SDP (Skill Development Program)

SDP focuses on improving technical and non-technical skills among students, preparing them for real-world challenges. These programs include workshops, certifications, and training modules tailored to industry standards.

EDI (Entrepreneurship Development Initiative)

EDI encourages students to develop entrepreneurial skills, providing resources, mentorship, and platforms to convert ideas into viable business models.

DT (Design Thinking)

DT introduces students to creative problem-solving techniques. It is centered on understanding user needs, prototyping solutions, and iterating based on feedback.

Course Projects

Course projects offer hands-on experience, enabling students to apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios. They are critical for understanding the application of concepts learned during coursework.
